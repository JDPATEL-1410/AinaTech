Our Story

How AinaTech started and where we're heading

2020

The Beginning

Founded by a team of passionate technologists who recognized the unique challenges faced by Indian SMEs in adopting digital solutions. Started with a vision to bridge the technology gap for small businesses.

2021

First Milestone

Launched our flagship CRM solution specifically designed for Indian MFDs and service businesses. Onboarded our first 50 clients and achieved 100% client retention rate.

2022

Expansion Phase

Expanded our services to include web design and digital marketing. Grew our team to 15+ professionals and opened our Mumbai office. Crossed 200+ successful projects.

2023

Innovation & Growth

Introduced AI-powered features in our CRM platform. Partnered with leading Indian businesses and achieved ISO 27001 certification for data security. Crossed 400+ clients milestone.

2024

Market Leadership

Became the preferred CRM solution provider for Indian MFDs and SMEs. Launched advanced automation features and mobile apps. Currently serving 500+ businesses across India.

2025

Future Vision

Expanding internationally while strengthening our position in India. Developing next-generation AI solutions and planning to serve 1000+ businesses by end of 2025.
